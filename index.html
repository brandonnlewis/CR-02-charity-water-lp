Questions, answered

Does 100% of my donation fund projects directly?

Yes — public donations are dedicated to the field. Separate funding covers operating expenses.

Can I track the exact project I supported?

Absolutely. We share updates, photos, and GPS coordinates as work progresses and completes.

Is my donation tax‑deductible?

Typically yes, if you’re donating to a qualified nonprofit in your country. Please consult your local guidelines.
